Welcome to Adrian's Workshop!

Adrian once lived in a small flat in London. He then decided to move his little family to West Wales, where suddenly he found himself owning a garage. Snubbing the car, Adrian decided to buy a bunch of tools so that he could fix things around his new house. And before he knew it, woodwork had charmed his socks off. He always had a passion for woodwork, from as early as grade seven, and now he's decided to seek profit from his newfound, dare he say, skill for the craft. Grab some safety gear and follow me!
